BlueWhale Hotel

BlueWhale Hotel has a total of 278 rooms and Suites 278 rooms and Suites(230 Deluxe, 1 Deluxe Room for the physically challenged, 39 Deluxe Plus, and 8 Suites). The hotel has 7 floors.
Its main facilities include an all-day dining restaurant, lobby lounge, room service open 06.00 – 23.00 Hrs, banquet and conference rooms, outdoor swimming pool with bar, fitness and both indoor and outdoor parking.
BlueWhale Hotel standards that are included in our facilities are complimentary WI-FI access in all our guest rooms and public areas, electronic key cards for additional safety and security, complimentary in-room tea, and coffee making facilities, business center, currency exchange.
The upscale Premier hotel offers refined atmosphere and style, deluxe features and amenities, superior confest and service – making your stay truly memorable.
A pre-reserve shuttle with full flight details is required.
The hotel operates Shuttle Van Services providing pre-scheduled journeys to and from Suvarnabhumi Airport
